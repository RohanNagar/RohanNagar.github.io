Pilot

Pilot is a cloud-management application. It can combine your files from Dropbox, Google Drive, Facebook, and more into one location. Development is currently in progress for Mac and will come to mobile in the future.

Thunder and Lightning

Thunder and Lightning are REST APIs that power the Pilot application. Thunder interfaces with a DynamoDB database to manage user information, while Lightning pulls files and data from various third party applications.

Space Invaders on an ARM board

In my second year at UT Austin, I participated in an embedded systems design contest. My friend and I programmed and wired up a version of the classic game Space Invaders in C. We won our class competiton and advanced to finals!

Stanford's Pintos Projects

During my Operating Systems course, myself and 2 others completed the series of Pintos projects created by Stanford, where we implemented Operating System constructs such as system calls, virtual memory, scheduling, and file systems.
